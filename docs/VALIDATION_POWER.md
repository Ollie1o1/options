_Generated by `scripts/validation_power_analysis.py`. Does not change the gate; it informs a human decision (see `status/DECISIONS.md`)._

## The honest bottom line

- To detect an effect of **IC≈0.10** (our best out-of-sample estimate) at p<0.05 with 80% power, you need **~783 closed trades** — not 50.
- At **n=50**, the smallest IC that is even significant at p<0.05 is **+0.279**. So the gate's `IC≥0.08 AND p<0.05` is really one rule: the `p<0.05` clause forces an observed IC of ~0.28 at n=50, far above 0.08. The `IC≥0.08` floor is non-binding at that sample size.
- **Implication:** n=50 will only ever fire READY if the *true* edge is strong (≳0.28). A real-but-modest edge (~0.10) cannot clear a fixed-n=50 frequentist gate — it would need hundreds of trades.

### Pooled-read caveat (why we don't just use the pool)
The pooled number mixes trades scored under different config/weight versions over time and includes calibration in-sample fitting — it is **not** a clean out-of-sample estimate. The walk-forward harness (`src/walk_forward.py`) remains the only leakage-controlled read. Treat the pool as a sanity check, not evidence.

## Recommendation

1. **Keep the n≥50 gate as the floor, but read it correctly.** At n=50 it is effectively a test for a *strong* edge (observed IC ≳ 0.28). That is a legitimate, conservative bar — if the screener is genuinely good the edge will show; if it's marginal, it won't clear, which is the honest outcome we want.
2. **Adopt the Bayesian posterior as the EXTEND/READY tie-breaker.** Once n≥50, instead of a brittle p-value cliff, also report P(true IC ≥ 0.08). Going live needs BOTH n≥50 AND posterior ≥ 0.85 (and the frequentist p<0.05 as the strong-edge confirmation). This avoids both false-go and endless waiting.
3. **Do NOT chase n≈800.** Detecting a 0.10 edge frequentist-clean would take ~2–3 years at this pace — not worth it. If the edge is only ~0.10, the correct decision is a *small* allocation justified by the Bayesian posterior and strict risk caps, not a large one justified by a p-value we can't reach.
4. **Action:** leave thresholds unchanged for now; revisit when the forward cohort reaches n≥50. This document is the basis for that future call.
